St. Joseph, Missouri

St. Joseph (or St.Joe) is a city in Missouri, United States. As of the 2010 census, St. Joseph had a total population of 76,780, making it the 8th largest city in the state. St. Joseph is located on the Missouri River, is best known as the starting point of the Pony Express and the death place of Jesse James. It is also home to Missouri Western State University.
